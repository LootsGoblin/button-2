The Button

What is The Button?

The Button is a mysterious entity that must be kept alive. It's connected to 10 lamps that slowly turn off over time.

How to Keep The Button Alive:

  • Watch the lamps - they will turn off one by one from left to right
  • Press the button to reset all lamps back to on
  • The lamps change color based on remaining time (Green → Yellow → Red)
  • Your score increases faster as more lamps turn off
  • Between 9 PM and 6 AM, The Button gets tired and moves at half speed
